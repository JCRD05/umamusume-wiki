Current events!
“The peak of confection” is here!

The new story event, The Peak of Confection, released on February 18, 2026, and will run until March 1, 2026. Participate to earn points and claim rewards!

Event rewards

  • Tosen Jordan (My Way) Speed card 400,000 points
  • Rainbow Crystal Shard x3 920,000 points
  • 1500 Carats 1,000,000 points

Is Tosen Jordan (My way) any good?

She's ok. She's on par for an SR card but it provides a useful gold skill. A good card for newer f2p players, as having a guaranteed MLB is great for those without large pools of speed cards.

Players will instead automatically receive event bonuses, even if the bonus Support Cards are not equipped. The value is based on the maximum uncap level of cards you own.
